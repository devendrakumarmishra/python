Why Multiple Regression?

Simple Regression: price = weight x area (only considers size)

Multiple Regression: price = w1 x area + w2 x bedrooms (considers both!)

A 2000 sq ft house with 2 bedrooms costs different from a 2000 sq ft house with 4 bedrooms. Multiple regression captures this.

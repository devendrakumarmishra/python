How This Works (Step by Step)

  1. Split into sentences — The text is broken into individual sentences
  2. Count word frequency — Words that appear often are considered important
  3. Score sentences — Each sentence gets a score based on its important words
  4. Pick top sentences — The highest-scoring sentences become the summary
  5. Reorder — Sentences are put back in original order so it reads naturally
